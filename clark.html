Clark Printing was a great experience because it had many exciting projects to do and had many talented artists to do them.
Once again, I designed circulars as well as store signs and more on a weekly basis. I helped everyone out that needed it and volunteered my time to increase production. I did advertisements for Metropolitan, Giunta’s and Pioneer Supermarkets (based in the NYC area) and had great success.
Unfortunately, that opportunity ended because they had to cut workforce by three to balance their books and I was one of the newest hires. I left on very good terms with a very possible return, but the Covid 19 Pandemic hit full swing everywhere in early 2020 as massive layoffs happened all over and it closed that glorious window for good for me to come back to Clark Printing.
