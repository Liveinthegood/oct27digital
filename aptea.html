I was a Senior Graphic Designer who was the top assistant to the manager and creative director on the production side of things. My job was to create and correct "print" circulars (and more) that promoted store sales on a weekly basis under tight deadlines. Fully armed, I managed a group of 10 artists when that time was needed and would report in to the production manager and creative director to keep them in the loop. The very busy production manager had to oversee the entire department, so he or she would delegate authority and certain powers to me to oversee their successful daily vision.
I also met with other stakeholders in other departments, spoke to printers and distributors when needed, trained staff and prepared documents for release when that time came. I also designed circular pages, ROP’s, bagstuffers, signage and even had some time for lunch when I wasn’t proofreading.
In the last year there in 2015, I took on the role of Production Manager. So it's pretty cool for me to live with the fact that I was the last Production Manager A&P ever had, dating back to 1859 when this proud company began.
It is important to note that the supermarkets Waldbaum's and Superfresh were part of the A&P Family of Supermarkets which I had a handle on as well doing the same responsibilities. Next Stop! Shoppers
